# Tutorial: Setting up call forwarding

## Step 1: Dial the service

From your handset, call 1-800-555-0190 to activate forwarding.

## Step 2: Enter the target number

Enter a 10-digit number like 2125550191, or a formatted one such as
(212) 555-0192.

## Step 3: Verify

Forward a test call to 212-555-0193. If it fails, call support at
212.555.0194.

## International forwarding

To forward abroad, prefix the country code, e.g. +44 20 1234 1234 or
+33 1 23 45 67 89. Local relay testing uses 555-0195.
