# Community Guidelines

Welcome! Please read these before posting.

## Reporting

See something off? Report it to moderators@community.example.org. Urgent
safety issues: safety+urgent@community.example.org.

## Appeals

If your account was actioned, appeal by emailing appeals@community.example.org
within 14 days. Appeals from the EU may also go to dpo@community.example.de
(our data protection officer).

## Don't share private contact info

Posting someone's address (like `victim at example dot com`) without
consent is not allowed.

## Staff

Reach the whole staff team at staff@community.example.org.
