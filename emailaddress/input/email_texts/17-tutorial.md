# Tutorial: Setting up email notifications

This guide shows how to wire up notifications.

## Step 1: Configure the sender

Set the `from` address to noreply@notifications.example.com. Do **not**
use a personal address here.

## Step 2: Add recipients

Add team members, e.g. ops@example.com and dev-alerts@example.com. You can
also use a plus-tag per service: alerts+billing@example.com.

## Step 3: Test

Send a test to your own address. If you don't receive it, email
deliverability@support.example.com.

## Troubleshooting

If DNS looks wrong, the registrar's support is reachable at
help@registrar.io or, on their status page, support at registrar dot io.
